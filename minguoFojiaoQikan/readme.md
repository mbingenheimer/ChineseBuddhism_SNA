The dataset made available here models an epistolary network of Chinese Buddhists in the Republican period (1912-1949).
The data is based on 2507 letters that were published between 1913 and 1947 in c. 50 different periodicals collected in the Minguo fojiao qikan jicheng 民國佛教期刊集成. Preliminary identification of epistolary items was possible by the [Catalog Database of Republican Era Buddhist Journals](https://buddhistinformatics.dila.edu.tw/minguofojiaoqikan/) at Dharma Drum, Taiwan.
The actual data collection (identification of sender(s) and addressee(s), de-duplication etc.) was made possible by a 2022 Temple University College of Liberal Arts Research Award (CLARA).
