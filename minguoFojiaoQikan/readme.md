# Epistolary Network of Republican Period Chinese Buddhism
The dataset models an epistolary network of Chinese Buddhists in the Republican period (1912-1949). The data is based on 2507 letters that were published between 1913 and 1947 in c. 50 different periodicals collected in the Minguo fojiao qikan jicheng 民國佛教期刊集成. It comprises 1102 actors (individuals and organizations) and 2833 connections.

Preliminary identification of epistolary items was made possible by the [Catalog Database of Republican Era Buddhist Journals](https://buddhistinformatics.dila.edu.tw/minguofojiaoqikan/) at Dharma Drum, Taiwan. The actual data collection (identification and disambiguation of sender(s) and addressee(s), de-duplication, cleanup, etc.) was made possible by a 2022 [Temple University College of Liberal Arts](https://liberalarts.temple.edu/) Research Award (CLARA).
